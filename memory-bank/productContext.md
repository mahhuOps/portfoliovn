# Product Context

**Problem:** Professionals need a way to showcase their work and skills in a visually appealing and effective manner. Building a portfolio from scratch can be time-consuming and require technical expertise.

**Solution:** Portfolio Manager provides an AI-powered platform that simplifies the portfolio creation process. Users can leverage AI to generate content, deploy to custom domains, and export professional CVs.

**User Experience Goals:**

*   Intuitive and easy-to-use interface
*   Visually appealing and professional designs
*   Seamless deployment and management
*   Fast and reliable performance
